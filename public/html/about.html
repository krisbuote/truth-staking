About

What is Truth Staking?


Truth staking is crowdsourcing the truth.

In this new free market, anyone can stake cryptocurrency on claims made by the media. Instead of relying on political pundits, truth staking enables the crowd to speculate on the honesty of the media and provide a fact-checking service. Players are rewarded for fact-checking, and content producers are rewarded for creating quality content.

How Does it Work?

Truth staking is run on the ethereum blockchain and managed entirely by smart contracts.

Anyone can stake cryptocurrency on claims made by the media as being true or false. All of the money staked is stored in a smart contract. At the end of the staking period, the sums of cryptocurrency placed on True and False are counted. The larger pot wins. The losing pot's money is divvied up and delivered to the winners. The larger your stake is, the larger your share of the winnings. All of this is done algorithmically via a smart contract which can be viewed here. Because Truth Staking exists on the blockchain, it is censorship-resistant. No one can control the outcome other than by putting their money on the line.

Why?

Truth staking is an attempt to restore media from the clutches of clickbait.

Since the advent of the internet, the cost of sharing information has rapidly been approaching zero. Information wants to be free, but it is not free to produce. As a result, most content producers, such as journalists, rely entirely on advertisers to earn a fair living. The ad-based economic model is rewarding clicks and views, not factual content. This economic model has led to the downstream effect of clickbait, outrage, fake news, and creepy data collection.

Truth staking proposes a new economic model: content producers subject their claims to the crowd and are rewarded directly with ethereum.

Truth staking is a return to a freer, more transparent, peer-to-peer information economy.

What's the Best Strategy?

The game rewards peer-consensus. Because you don't know how your peers will behave, the best strategy is to stake honestly with what you believe to be true. Furthermore, it is in your best interest to research the claim, because that's what other players will be doing. From a game theory perspective, there is a focal point on the truth.
