About

What is Truth Staking?

Truth staking is crowdsourcing the truth.

In this new free market, anyone can stake cryptocurrency on claims made by the media. Instead of relying on political pundits, truth staking enables the crowd to speculate on the honesty of the media and provide a fact-checking service. Players earn Ether for fact-checking, and content producers are rewarded for creating quality content.

How Does it Work?

Truth Staking is like competitive crowdfunding for the truth.

  • Individuals submit claims made by the media (and earn Ether for doing so)
  • The crowd stakes Ether on the claim being True or False
  • When the staking period ends, the money is counted and the majority position wins
  • Players who staked with the majority win the money staked by the players in the minority
  • Larger stakes earn bigger returns. For example, if you stake twice as much as your friend, you will receive twice the return. See FAQ.

The money management is done automatically with a smart contract which can be viewed here. Because Truth Staking exists on the Ethereum blockchain, it is censorship-resistant. No one can control the outcome other than by putting their money on the line. Truth Staking uses MetaMask to secure all transactions.

Why?

Truth staking is an attempt to restore media from the clutches of clickbait.

Since the advent of the internet, the cost of sharing information has rapidly been approaching zero. Information wants to be free, but it is not free to produce. As a result, most content producers, such as journalists, rely entirely on advertisers to earn a fair living. The ad-based economic model is rewarding clicks and views, not factual content. This economic model has led to the downstream effect of clickbait, outrage, fake news, and creepy data collection.

Truth staking proposes a new economic model: content producers subject their claims to the crowd and are rewarded directly with Ether.

Truth staking is a return to a freer, more transparent, peer-to-peer information economy.

What's the Best Strategy?

The game rewards peer-consensus. Because you don't know how your peers will behave, the best strategy is to stake honestly with what you believe to be true. Furthermore, it is in your best interest to research the claim, because that's what other players will be doing. From a game theory perspective, there is a focal point on the truth.

FAQ

The reward for winning stakers is calculated as the relative size of stake multiplied by the size of the losing pot.

For example:

  1. Person A stakes 2 Ether on True.
  2. Person B stakes 3 Ether on False.
  3. Person C stakes 4 Ether on True.
The winning pot (True) contains 6 Ether. The losing pot (False) contains 3 Ether. Because Person A contributed 1/3rd of the winning pot, he receives 1/3rd of the rewards (1/3 * 3 Ether = 1 Ether). Because Person C contributed 2/3rds of the winning pot, she receives 2/3rds of the rewards (2/3 * 3 Ether = 2 Ether).

Anyone who submits a new claim, the "market maker", will receive the "market maker's reward", regardless of the outcome. The market maker's reward is calculated as the relative size of their first stake compared to the total pot.

For example, if the market maker submits a new claim with an initial stake of 1 Ether, and the total amount of Ether staked is 10 Ether, the market maker will receive a guaranteed reward of 10% of the losing pot.

The market maker will then receive the regular rewards as calculated by the rules of the game. See "How are the rewards calculated?".

Truth Staking inherits all the benefits of Ethereum's blockchain: transparency, security, and censorship-resistance.

  1. Transparency: The money is automatically managed by a computer program (the smart contract) which is completely visible to all.
  2. Security: Truth Staking (the web application) is never in control of anyone's money, ever. There is no need to trust Truth Staking, it's simply a convenient interface for the smart contract. Truth Staking uses MetaMask to provide secure transactions.
  3. Censorship-resistance: Because truth staking exists on the blockchain, no one can manipulate or hide the content and results. It is completely determined by the rules of the game. Truth staking is a grass-roots movement with bottom up control.
