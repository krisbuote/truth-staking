What is Truth Staking?

Truth Staking is the creation of a new free market. In this market, anyone can stake cryptocurrency on claims made by the media.

Instead of relying on political pundits, Truth Staking enables the crowd to speculate on the honesty of the media and provide a fact-checking service. In return, they receive a reward for doing so. Truth Staking is a new free market on media; players are rewarded for fact-checking, and content producers are rewarded for creating great content.

How Does it Work?

Anyone can stake cryptocurrency on claims made by the media as being true or false. All of the money staked on a claim is stored in a smart contract. At the end of the staking period, the sums of cryptocurrency placed on True and False are counted. The larger pot wins. The losing pot's money is divvied up and delivered to the winners. The larger your stake is, the larger your share of the winnings. All of this is done algorithmically via a smart contract which can be viewed here. Because Truth Staking exists on the blockchain, it is transparent and censorship-resistant. No one can control the outcome other than by putting their money on the line.

Why?

Since the advent of the internet, the cost of sharing information has rapidly been approaching zero. Information wants to be free, but it is not free to produce. As a result, most content producers now rely entirely on advertisers to earn a decent living. The current economic model for information is incentvizing clicks and views. This incentivization structure has led to the downstream effect of clickbait, outrage, fake news, and creepy data collection. Truth Staking is an attempt to get back to the peer-to-peer information economy.

What's the Best Strategy?

The game rewards peer-consensus. Because you don't know how your peers will behave, the best strategy is to stake honestly with what you believe to be true. Furthermore, it is in your best interest to research the claim, because that's what other players will be doing. From a game theory perspective, there is a focal point on the truth.
